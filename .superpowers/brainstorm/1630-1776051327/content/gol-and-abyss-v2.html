New

Cellular Divinity

Conway's Game of Life seeded with known interesting patterns — Acorn, R-pentomino, gliders, oscillators. Cells are colored by age (bright at birth → deep at maturity) and each newborn inherits a hue blended from its living neighbors, so color propagates through the colony like genetic information. Bass triggers new pattern injections. When the population collapses, new seeds scatter.

Revised

Bioluminescent Abyss v2

Darker, stranger. Jellyfish replaced by slowly rotating abstract radial forms — pulsing halo-rings with branching filaments, like deep-sea creatures that have no name. Drifting thread-curtains of micro-light instead of distinct particles. Occasional cold detonations of bioluminescence from the abyss floor. No recognizable anatomy.
